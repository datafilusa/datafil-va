About Us

At DataFil, we understand the pressure and chaos that come with running your own business. As entrepreneurs ourselves, we’ve experienced the sleepless nights and the ongoing struggle to find reliable support.

We know how hard it is to find assistance that truly understands the unique needs of entrepreneurs. Hiring takes time, and losing staff can set you back even more. This experience is what led to the creation of DataFil to provide professional virtual assistants trained to adapt to your needs and lighten your load.

With a global team of skilled professionals, our mission is simple: we endeavor to make your life easier and your business thrive no matter the industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DataFil?

DataFil provides professional, dedicated virtual assistants to help you run your business more efficiently no matter the industry or size.

How is a DataFil VA different from regular admin support?

Our VAs are exclusively assigned to your business, adapting to your workflow and offering consistent, personalized support.

What tasks can a DataFil VA handle?

We cover administrative work, data entry, email management, scheduling, customer service, reports, social media, and even medical-related tasks.

Is DataFil a good fit for my industry?

Yes! We support real estate, healthcare, education, e-commerce, and more with trained VAs who understand your field.

How do I get started?

Go to our Contact Us page and describe your needs. We'll match you with the perfect assistant.

Do I get to choose my assistant?

We’ll match you based on your needs, and you can be involved in the final selection if desired.

Is support available 24/7?

Yes, our VAs ensure around-the-clock support based on your business hours and timezone.

Can I scale my support if my business grows?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, add VAs, or adjust services anytime based on your needs.

Why choose DataFil over hiring directly?

We save you time and risk offering trained, pre-vetted VAs, fast onboarding, and ongoing quality assurance so you can focus on growing your business.

Is hiring a VA from DataFil affordable?

Our services are affordable for international clients and provide sustainable income for Filipino VAs. We believe in fair pay that improves quality of life while delivering reliable support without the complexity and high costs of in-house staffing.
